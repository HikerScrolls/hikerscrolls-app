Powered by AI

Intelligence built into every journal

Bring your API key for Gemini or Claude. HikerScrolls uses AI to enrich your journals with artwork, context, and smart layouts.

✏️
Pen Sketch Generation

Every photo can be transformed into a clean pen-and-ink illustration using Gemini's image generation. Sketches are saved and shown alongside the original photo.

📍
Location Enrichment

AI analyzes your photos to identify the location, look up historical context, and write a compelling travel narrative — all with one click during the journal wizard.

🗂️
Smart Layout

The AI layout engine intelligently assigns your photos and notes to journal sections, ordering them by your GPX track to preserve the natural flow of your journey.

🗺️
EXIF GPS Mapping

Upload photos with GPS metadata and the plugin automatically plots them on the route map — no manual pinning required.

🌐
Multi-Model Support

Use Gemini 2.0 Flash for vision and image generation, Claude for nuanced text analysis, and Tavily for real-time web search — all configurable in plugin settings.

🔒
Private & Local-First

Your journal data lives in your Obsidian vault. AI calls go directly from your device to the respective API — no intermediary server ever sees your content.

How It Works

From trip to story in three steps

The journal wizard walks you through everything. No design skills needed.

1

Name your trip, pick a date range, upload a GPX file, and drop in your photos. The wizard handles EXIF GPS extraction automatically.

2

Let AI identify locations, write descriptions, generate pen sketches from photos, and arrange everything into sections ordered by your route.

3

Get Started

Install in 60 seconds

HikerScrolls is a community plugin for Obsidian. No external account needed to get started.

  • 1
    Open Obsidian and go to Settings → Community Plugins → Browse
  • 2
    Search for HikerScrolls and click Install
  • 3
    Enable the plugin and paste your Gemini API key in the settings panel
  • 4
    Click the HikerScrolls icon in the left sidebar to open the library and create your first journal
📋 Requirements
  • Obsidian v1.0 or later
  • Gemini API key (free tier available)
  • Trip photos (JPG/PNG)
  • GPX file from your hike or ride
  • Optional: Claude API key for richer text
  • Optional: Tavily API key for web search
